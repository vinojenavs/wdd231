Site Plan: Delta Fresh Produce

Site Name

Delta Fresh Produce

This name was chosen because it clearly represents fresh farm produce from Delta State, Nigeria.

Optional domain: deltafreshproduce.com.ng

Site Purpose

The site will connect farmers and consumers by showing seasonal produce, market prices, and delivery options. It will also include a simple contact form for buyers.

Scenarios

  • Where can I see current prices for yam and cassava?
  • How do I list my farm on the site?

Color Scheme

Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) – used for headings.

Warm Orange (#F59E0B) – used for highlights and accents.

Typography

Headings: Arial Bold

Body: Arial Regular

Wireframe (Simple Sketch)

Mobile view:

Logo → Menu → Hero Image → Produce List → Contact Form → Footer

Desktop view:

Header (Logo + Nav) → Hero Banner → Two Columns (Produce Info | Delivery Info) → Contact → Footer
